MILKY MIST

Milky Mist Dairy Food Private Limited (MMD), one of India's largest manufacturers of dairy products, is located at Perundurai, 20 km (12 mi) from the Erode district in Tamil Nadu. Formed in 1997 by T. Sathish Kumar, Milky Mist is engaged in milk procurement, processing, and manufacturing of other dairy products.
